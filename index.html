About Me

I graduated from CU Boulder in 2012 with a degree in Broadcast Production. Since then I have been working in bars and restaurants and traveling the world. I bought a one way ticket to Europe in 2014 and came home only twice over four years. Along the way I picked up any job I could to get by and save money. Staying in one place for longer than a couple weeks allows you to really understand the culture and local way of life. In the summer of 2016 I completed my Yoga Teacher Training in Rishikesh, India and taught yoga on the South coast of India for two years after. I am back in Colorado, with my Indian street dog that I rescued, ready to challenge myself and take a new direction in my life!
