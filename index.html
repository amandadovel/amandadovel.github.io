MuziqueHub❌

MuziqueHub is a MERN stack web application designed for connecting users to information on their favorite artists. After searching for a band or musician the user is provided with a short biography, images, social media links and even music videos. They can also search by artist or location to find relevant concerts. The app is available to anyone but only after logging in the user will be able to save favorites to their own personal feed.
